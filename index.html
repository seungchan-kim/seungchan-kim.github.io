Seungchan Kim

email: seungch2@andrew.cmu.edu

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Sebastian Scherer. My research focuses on spatial reasoning and semantic representations to advance robot exploration and navigation, with applications to UAVs and multi-robot systems.

I am also a robotics research intern at Field AI.

Previously, I earned a B.S. in Applied Mathematics & Computer Science and a M.S. in Computer Science from Brown University, where I was advised by George Konidaris and Michael Littman. At Brown, my research centered on deep reinforcement learning.
