Seungchan Kim

I am an incoming PhD student in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science. My research interests are at the intersection of reinforcement learning and robotics.

Previously, I received B.S. in Applied Mathematics & Computer Science and M.S. in Computer Science from Brown University. During my time at Brown, I worked with Professor George Konidaris, Michael Littman, and Stefanie Tellex on research in deep reinforcement learning and POMDP planning.
