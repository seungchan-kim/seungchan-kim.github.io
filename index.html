Seungchan Kim

I am a master's student studying computer science at Brown University. Previously, I earned my B.S. degree in Applied Mathematics & Computer Science at Brown. I am now mainly advised by Professor George Konidaris at Intelligent Robot Lab (IRL). I also work with Professor Michael Littman at Reinforcement Learning & Adaptive Behavior (RLAB) group and Professor Stefanie Tellex at Humans to Robots (H2R) Lab.

My research interests are artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), specifically reinforcement learning (RL). I am interested in building general-purpose intelligent systems that perform well on a wide range of tasks and reason/plan in long-horizon, sparse-reward problems. My previous research in AI/ML has spanned the topics of computer vision, deep RL, model-based RL, and planning in POMDPs.
